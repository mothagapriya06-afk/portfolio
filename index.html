My Process

How I approach
every problem

A structured, user-centred methodology that turns ambiguity into clarity.

1
🔍
Step 01
Discover & Research

I immerse myself in the user's world — stakeholder interviews, competitor analysis, and contextual research — before touching any tools.

2
🗺️
Step 02
Define & Structure

Synthesising research into clear user personas, journey maps, and information architecture — establishing the design foundation.

3
✏️

Design & Prototype

From wireframes to high-fidelity Figma designs, I craft visuals that balance aesthetics with usability — then bring them to life with interactive prototypes.
